# Property-price
Comprehensive Real Estate Market Analysis & Sales Dashboard
1. Executive Financial Summary:

Total Market Valuation: A robust total sales volume of $2,597,867,440.

Average Market Price: Properties average around $4,766,729.25.

Price Spectrum: Analysis covers a wide range from a minimum of $1.75M to a maximum of $13.3M.

2. Property Attributes & Feature Insights:

Essential Amenities: Tracks the availability of Air Conditioning and Guestrooms through interactive donut charts.

Infrastructure & Access: Visualizes the percentage of properties with Mainroad access and specific Parking capacities (0 to 3 slots).

Capacity Analysis: Categorizes units based on the number of Bedrooms (1 to 6) and total Stories.

3. Strategic Market Segmentation:

Price Classification: Groups properties into Budget, Normal, and Luxury tiers for targeted analysis.

Furnishing & Area Correlation: A specialized combo chart evaluates how furnishing status (Furnished, Semi-furnished, Unfurnished) impacts the relationship between price and total area.
4. Interactive Analysis Tools:

Dynamic Slicers: Integrated filters for Price, Bedrooms, and Stories allowing for real-time data exploration and custom reporting.
